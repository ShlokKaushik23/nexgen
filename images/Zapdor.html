WHO WE ARE

Company Overview

Zapdor Engineering is an emerging EPC company comprising of professionals that provides independent expertise in designing and project management for its clients in Railway Infrastructure domain and related areas to governments, industries and others.

Managed by a group of highly trained professionals with multidisciplinary skills and a wide spectrum of experience in India; Zapdor Engineering undertakes several assignments on turnkey basis including Project Development, Design Management, Project Management, Cost Management, Value Engineering in Railway Electrification sector.

The company has a well equipped and trained team of Electrical, Civil, Mechanical and IT professionals on its rolls. Our team of experienced and responsive engineers & skilled manpowers provides a "one stop" approach for total project delivery, offering clients engineering solutions that satisfy their cost and quality requirements.

We have kept our focus on the electrification sector of Railways, keeping in view the vast scope of work in Railway infrastructure sector.
